Our Trip

Travel Adventure

It is a big and beautiful world out there. Most of us live and die in the same corner where we are born and never get to see any of it. WE DON’T WANT THAT TO HAPPEN TO YOU. We at Odyssey want to take away the pain of planning the holidays. But the catch is, the destination remains a surprise. We can find you the most beautiful destinations and hidden gems to explore.

Why Choose Us?

The Problem

Want to get away for a weekend but have no time to plan? What if you don't know where you want to go?

The Idea

What if you want to be surprised?

The Surprise

We plan 3-day weekend trips around India. All you have to do is tell us your budget and fill out a quick survey. We'll take care of your travel + accommodation arrangements - all while keeping your destination a surprise.

How It Works

Step 1

Sign up and answer a few questions so that we get to know you better. Our team will match your travel preferences with an ideal location for you.

Step 2

Within 3 working days we will get back to you with multiple budget options for your trip.Once you authorize a budget, we will go ahead and confirm your bookings for your surprise destination.

Step 3

A week before your departure we will send you an email which contains weather forecast and recommended items to pack. 48 hours prior to departure we will send you an email which reveals your destination.

Step 4

Thats It!!! Just get your bag and go.
